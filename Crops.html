Crops Cultivated in India

Crop Image Region Season Details
Rice North and East India Kharif
Advantages: High yield, staple food, source of income for farmers.
Disadvantages: Requires a lot of water, pests and diseases may affect crops.
Wheat North India Rabi
Advantages: Easy to store, rich in carbohydrates, supports rural economy.
Disadvantages: Susceptible to pests, requires cold temperature.
Barley North India Rabi
Advantages: Drought resistant, used for food and animal feed.
Disadvantages: Requires cool temperatures, susceptible to fungal diseases.
Tea Assam, Darjeeling, Nilgiri Hills Annual
Advantages: High demand in the global market, provides employment.
Disadvantages: Labour-intensive, requires a specific climate.
Coffee Karnataka, Kerala Annual
Advantages: High export potential, good for the economy.
Disadvantages: Needs specific climatic conditions, can be prone to diseases like leaf rust.
Jute West Bengal, Bihar Summer
Advantages: Used in textiles and bags, biodegradable.
Disadvantages: Requires a lot of water, prone to disease.
Groundnut Gujarat, Andhra Pradesh Kharif
Advantages: Rich in protein, used in cooking oil production.
Disadvantages: Sensitive to drought, susceptible to pests.
Mustard North India Rabi
Advantages: Rich in oil content, helps in soil fertilization.
Disadvantages: Requires moderate rainfall, prone to fungal diseases.
Potato Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal Rabi
Advantages: Easy to store, widely consumed, versatile.
Disadvantages: Prone to blight, requires cool and moist conditions.
Ginger Kerala, West Bengal Annual
Advantages: Used in cooking and medicine, high market demand.
Disadvantages: Needs high humidity and temperature, susceptible to rhizome rot.
Onion Haryana, Maharashtra Kharif
Advantages: High demand, used in cooking globally.
Disadvantages: Prone to pests, needs specific temperature and soil conditions.
Tomato Madhya Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh Kharif
Advantages: Rich in vitamins, high demand.
Disadvantages: Sensitive to diseases, needs careful irrigation.
Carrot Punjab, Uttar Pradesh Rabi
Advantages: Rich in vitamin A, highly nutritious.
Disadvantages: Sensitive to drought, requires well-drained soil.
Chili Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu Kharif
Advantages: High market value, used in various cuisines.
Disadvantages: Requires high temperature, susceptible to pests.
